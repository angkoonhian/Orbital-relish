About Relish

Our project aims to recruit stay-home chefs, be it aspiring teenagers who want to dabble in cooking or stay-home spouses that want to showcase their ethnic cuisine, and to connect these chefs to a wider customer base that would rather home-cooked food rather than what is readily available out there now.

People love home-cooked food!


You prepare it


We deliver it.

ADDITIONAL REVENUE

Earn additional revenue by doing things you love with Relish! People across the nation are looking for heart-warming home-cooked food!

FLEXIBLE SCHEDULE

Pause orders if you're feeling overwhelmed. You decide for yourself when you want to cook for your food lovers and are free to leave on a monthly basis.
